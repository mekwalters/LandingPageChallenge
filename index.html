What is a responisive website?

A responsive website adjusts the pages size and format to fit the screen. It keeps your site looking professional whether it is viewed on a desktop, laptop or phone.

Having a responsive website provides you with a few advantages. First, it will improve a user's experience regardless of what type of device they use to access your website. Second, it will improve your visibility to search engines. Third, it provides a standard experience across devices, helping people recognize your brand.
